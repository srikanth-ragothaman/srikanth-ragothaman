## 🎯 Current Mission: AyurCDS

Founder  of **[AyurCDS](https://ayurcds.com)** — a point-of-care **Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS)** for Ayurvedic medicine.

### Why AyurCDS?
Ayurvedic clinical wisdom is rich, multidimensional, and deeply documented across classical texts, case literature, and contemporary research. However, its fragmented nature makes real-time point-of-care retrieval challenging. 

**Our Mission:**
* **Empower Clinicians:** Provide real-time pattern matching and differential diagnostic support to reduce clinical uncertainty.
* **Advance Informatics:** Build interoperable taxonomy and structured datasets to foster integrative healthcare research and evidence-based validation.
